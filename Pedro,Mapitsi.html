Sinopsis

Pedro "Mapitsi" Pérez Martínez, as an indigenous businessman, who lives constantly paradoxes, swings between having to satisfy the stereotypes of what that should be in the tourism industry, and his aspirations to be a recognized entrepreneur. Pedro's leadership and management skills are such that allowed us to produce, through its experience, an overview of the tourist industry in La Merced. The audiovisual product is called "Pedro, Mapitsi "because everything revolves around Pedro and his seek by his name, by his indigeneity.
